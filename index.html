Planning for the Future
a. Smart cities and Urban or Rural logistics
From the experiences of the forgotten to the predictions beyond our time, our past and present have presented us with the torch of a safer and smarter future. It is up to our participants to set light to the fire that keeps us alive, and provide a feasible solution to the urban and rural problems.

b. Normalizing the new normal
Normalcy as a concept is extremely subjective. Normalcy is a cold molecule that keeps changing its shape. It is now in the participants’ hands to normalize the new normal in a way that it fits all lines of the jigsaw that this society has carved. This addresses the changes in lifestyle that Covid has forced us to mould into.
Social
a. Non-profit
Altruism, an investment made by a person that pays back in the form of someone else’s smile. No act is truly selfless because even doing something for someone else brings us comfort. This is our participants’ chance to help the society by coming up with viable solutions to the day to day problems faced by the ones in need.

b. Cyber Security
The “internet”, humans are gifted with a vast platform to discover many hidden aspects of their minds. Often this curiosity and brilliance lead to cyber crimes, the dark side of the internet. It is now the participants’ task to bathe this dark corner in the light of security.
Fintech
One of our country's newest fronts finding its place in the ever expanding global economy is finance, next to that is technology. We are here to make the two meet and boost the growth even more. How the participants do that is in their own hands.
Emerging Technologies
This is a track where our hackers can run without any defined direction and there are no limits. They’re required to explore the trending technologies and use them to solve real world problems.

ABOUT

HackBattle’20 is a 36 hour Hack-a-Thon where Tomorrow's Developers are pitted against each other in a series of tracks that test their skills in the fields of Design, Web and Mobile App Development, Machine Learning, Blockchain and so much more. Apart from this, we also have keynote sessions by speakers from reputed companies, expert guidance by mentors and several fun activities, with chances to win exciting prizes!
